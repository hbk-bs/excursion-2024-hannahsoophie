Observing the outside world

Monday 10am -
A stroll through Leipzig's city center.
Accompanied by our sketchbooks, the gray walls seem less gray.
What seemed normal is suddenly new and interesting.
I am the decisionmaker of what I want to make visible.

A group of tourists stand together and listen to a city guide
who tells them about the city's past.
A man tries in vain to open the lock on his bicycle.
Colors and shapes rush past us,
as we try to capture all that seems worthy to us.
